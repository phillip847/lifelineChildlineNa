Welcome to Couselling Center

Where Best Counseling is offered.

Lifeline Childline (LLCL) Namibia is a registered Welfare Organization and NGO whose vision is safer, healthier, more resilient children, families and communities in Namibia. Lifeline Childline operates a 24/7 National Counselling Centre in Windhoek, which offers free telephonic, SMS, face to face and online Counselling services amongst other things .

LifeLine/ChildLine Namibia currently runs the only national 24/7 helpline based counselling service in Namibia. In addition to the HelpLines that are aimed at ensuring children are safe, the Counselling Centre also does face-to-face counselling, Community and schoolâ€™s outreach programmes.
